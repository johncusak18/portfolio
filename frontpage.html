mads - 23 - copenhagen

I am 23 year old guy from Copenhagen Denmark, i love the graphic and digital world. Most of my work nowadays involve frontend development and/or graphic design. On this site i will give you a look in to what i do, have done and what i like doing. I did not study webdevelopment but something else called multimediadesign. Multimediadesign gave me the opportunity to learn about graphic design, videoediting and much more. All these things are indeed very usable when you work with frontend development. Recently i went to work an internship in New York for a digital agency called Vertic. This was an incredible experience, and I will for sure come back to the city that never sleeps. I also really like videofilming/editing, and on the next page you can see some of things i've done. I usually make music videos for my good friends at Loegposen Records(where i am also co-founder). I love music(who doesn't?), mostly hiphop and electronic music, but over the last couple of years i've really started to love the 'funk era'. I am also a really big MJ fan, and despite most 23 year old people I have seen him perfom no more than 3 times! RIP, to the king of pop, no one can ever replace him!
